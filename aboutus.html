  About Our Chairman

 

  Hello everyone, and welcome to our school community. I am Harshit Rana, and I am    honored to be your new chairman. I come to you with 15 years of experience in education, including 10 years as a chairman. I am passionate about creating a safe and supportive learning environment where all students can thrive. I believe that every student has the potential to succeed, and it is my job to create a school where all students feel supported and challenged. I am committed to working with all stakeholders, including students, parents, teachers, and staff, to create a school that is a source of pride for our community.
